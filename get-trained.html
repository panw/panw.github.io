Courses

My team provides curated training for individuals and businesses to understand and leverage the latest technological breakthroughs. Our focus is on fundamental tools for AI/machine learning, blockchain, design, web, mobile and scalable cloud infrastructure development. Instructors are industry experts from Stanford University, Microsoft Research, Microsoft, Cisco, NASA and more. All intro courses have no prerequisites, which means that anyone from any background can attend. You can take courses in any order, as each are designed without prerequisites. Please contact me with any question on Twitter: @itspanw or Email: itspanw@gmail.com.
